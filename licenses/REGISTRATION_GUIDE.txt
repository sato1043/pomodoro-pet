REGISTRATION GUIDE

Pomodoro Pet


1. ABOUT POMODORO PET

Pomodoro Pet is a 3D virtual pet Pomodoro timer. A cute character
walks alongside you while you work, helping you stay focused and
take proper breaks.


2. FREE TRIAL

You can use all features for free for 30 days from the first launch.
No registration or payment is required during the trial period.


3. AFTER THE TRIAL

After the trial period ends, the basic Pomodoro timer and character
display remain available. Some features (settings customization,
statistics, fureai mode, weather, sound presets, etc.) will be
limited until you register.


4. REGISTRATION BENEFITS

Registering unlocks all features permanently:
- Full timer settings customization
- Statistics and history
- Fureai (interaction) mode
- Weather and time-of-day settings
- Sound presets and volume control
- Background notifications
- Automatic updates
- Offline use (up to 30 days without internet)


5. HOW TO REGISTER

Step 1: Purchase Pomodoro Pet on itch.io
        https://www.updater.cc

Step 2: Find your Download Key
        After purchase, you will receive a Download Key.
        It can be found in:
        - The purchase confirmation page (in the download URL)
        - The purchase confirmation email from itch.io

Step 3: Enter the Download Key in the app
        Open Pomodoro Pet, go to Settings, click "Register",
        and paste your Download Key. Click "Register" to complete.


6. WHERE TO FIND YOUR DOWNLOAD KEY

On itch.io, after purchasing, go to your purchase page:
  My purchases > Pomodoro Pet > Download

The URL of the download page contains your key:
  https://OWNER.itch.io/GAME/download/XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX
                                       ^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
                                       This is your Download Key

You can also find it in the purchase confirmation email from itch.io.

- サウンドプリセットと音量制御
